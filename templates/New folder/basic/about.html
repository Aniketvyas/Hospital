Our Facilities

PATIENT

Patient can search doctor by doctor’s degree or specification, doctor’s name or clinic’s name. Patient can utilize his/her time rather than going at early in the morning to take appointment and wait till the number came. Secret information of patient is not visible to other patient; privacy of the patient is maintained.

DOCTOR

Doctor can see the personal information and allergic information of patient, and depending upon the allergy, doctor can give him prescription. Doctor can also view the patient history. There is no need of paper to keep record of patient.
